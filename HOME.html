About the Conference

The 1  International Conference on Innovations and Future scope of Mechanical Engineering in Digital Era will be held during 20 - 21 December, 2024, at Anurag University Hyderabad. Importance and Scope of Conference

The International Conference on Innovations and Future Scope of Mechanical Engineering in the Digital Era (ICIFMEDE-24) holds significant importance in the realm of Mechanical Engineering due to its focus on innovation and future prospects within the digital landscape. This conference serves as a platform for experts, researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to converge and exchange ideas, insights, and advancements in Mechanical Engineering within the context of the digital era.

One of the key important features of ICIFMEDE-24 lies in its emphasis on Innovation. As Technology continues to evolve at a rapid pace, it is imperative for the field of Mechanical Engineering to stay abreast of the latest developments and advancements. This conference provides a Platform to discuss innovative approaches, methodologies, and technologies that can revolutionize the field and drive progress in areas such as Automation, Robotics, Renewable Energy, Additive Manufacturing, and more.

ICIFMEDE-24 explores the future scope of Mechanical Engineering in the digital era. With the increasing integration of digital Technologies such Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), Big Data Analytics, and Virtual Reality, the landscape of Mechanical Engineering is underground profound transformations. This conference delves into the potential implications of these technologies on various aspects of Mechanical Engineering, including Design, Manufacturing, Maintenance, and Sustainability. With providing the knowledge in future trends and opportunities, ICIFMEDE-24 aims at valuable insights and guidance to researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to cruise towards the innovations in Mechanical Engineering.

About Anurag University

    Anurag University, formerly known as Anurag Group of Institutions, was founded in 2002 and has emerged as one of the leading universities in Telangana State. Renowned for its commitment to excellence, the university is highly regarded for its provision of technical education with rigorous academic standards. Anurag University is positioned within the 101-150 rank band in the University Category. It adopts Outcome Based Education, modern methodologies, and a blended pedagogical approach to empower students in achieving their aspirations.

About Department of Mechanical Engineering

      The Department of Mechanical Engineering was Established in 2004 and received accreditation from the NBA under Tier-I in 2015. Subsequently, it has been reaccredited twice, with validity extended until June 2025. The department has a team of 37 qualified and dedicated faculty members,out of which 13 Doctorates and 24 pursuing Ph.Ds with various Specializations. Through Outcome Based Education (OBE) and Project Based Learning (PBL) models, students are offered a comprehensive blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Additionally, the department offers value-added courses in ANSYS and SOLIDWORKS, enhancing students' proficiency in these areas. With strong ties to the industry, it has established 19 Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with leading engineering companies. Moreover, it houses an exclusive library comprising over 300 volumes and e-journals, supports active professional chapters (ISTE, SAE, ISHRAE), and hosts student clubs such as the FEM, MARK, and CAD clubs.
